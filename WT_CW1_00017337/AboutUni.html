About Uzbek State World Language University

Uzbek State World Language University (USWLU) took its roots in 1949 year as Tashkent pedagogical foreign languages university. University has a – 70 – year history. In 1992 Tashkent pedagogical world languages university and Russian language and literature universities accompanied with each other and officially called Uzbek State World Language University. Nowadays approximately 9000 students are learning about 20 foreign languages

In the educational standards and setting of world. Every year in 15 educational speciality bachelors and 13 speciality masters degree are pursued. The students of university are sharpening the skills particularly language learning, teaching, translating and analyzing on the basis of acquired knowledge and practice. In our advancing world educational system also is changing day by day. That is to say that the connection between language and culture is considered to be the basic point in educational system of Higher education and university. Potential pedagogics of 1 , 2 and 3 faculties, Roman german filology, Russian faculties are learning the features of teaching the language while English, Korean, japan, and chineese languages translation are acquired in Theory and Practice of translation faculty. In university the students who occupied the skills of foreign language are cramming for in different fields nowadays. It is honourful that there are leading translators, foreign language teachers, politicians, and the diligent personnels of companies among the the graduates of university. In 2018 with the partnership of university of US , Webster a one year masters degree pursuing system was declared. This platform is considered to be the only in Central Asia, and it serves all youth around the world as a educational place. Main Tasks of Uzbek State University of World Languages

Task
1 Ensuring the organization of the educational process at a high level of efficiency and training of qualified personnel, in accordance with the state educational standard, based on innovative, modern educational and professional programs.
2 Educating young people based on the idea and ideology of National independence, in the spirit of humanism, knowledge of universal values, love for the Motherland, family and nature.
3 Using the success of developed countries, developing a new concept for training professionals in the official languages of the UN and its application.
4 Training teachers of foreign languages and translators for all educational structures.
5 Introduction of methods of intensive teaching of foreign languages with conducting various studies, and using computer technology, audiovisual means.
6 Training highly qualified international journalists.
7 Establishing direct links with foreign educational institutions in this area, drawing up a long-term partnership agreement.
8 To organize a fund of foreign currency by using its intellectual abilities.
9 To strengthen the material and technical base of the university and to enrich it.
10 To create conditions for the participation of professors and students in training courses created abroad to improve their qualifications and exchange experience.
11 To establish an exchange of students and faculty based on international relations.
12 To develop joint curricula and joint diploma programs with foreign universities.
13 To create new textbooks, teaching aids and programs using the experience of foreign universities.
