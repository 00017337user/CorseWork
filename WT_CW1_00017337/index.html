Uzbekistan state world languages university

Uzbekistan State University of World Languages (UzSWLU) in 1949 It started its activity under the name of the Tashkent Pedagogical Institute of Foreign Languages. The university is 70 years old has a history. In 1992, the Tashkent Pedagogical Institute of Foreign Languages and the Republic of Russian Language and The Institute of Literature merged and became the State University of World Languages of Uzbekistan.
