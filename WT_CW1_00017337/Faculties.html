Our Faculties

Faculty of English Philology

Focuses on the study of English language, literature, and teaching methodologies, preparing students for careers in linguistics and education.

First English Faculty

Provides comprehensive training in English language and translation, with an emphasis on language acquisition and cultural studies.

Second English Faculty

Specializes in English for specific purposes, including business, legal, and technical English, aiming to prepare versatile language professionals.

Third English Faculty

Offers advanced courses in English linguistics, translation theory, and intercultural communication.

Romano-Germanic Philology Faculty

Covers languages such as German, French, and Spanish, with a focus on philology and translation studies.

Faculty of Russian Philology

Provides in-depth study of the Russian language, literature, and linguistics, preparing students for careers in teaching and translation.

Oriental Philology Faculty

Focuses on Eastern languages and cultures, including Arabic, Persian, and Chinese, with strong emphasis on translation and cultural studies.

International Journalizm Faculty

Prepares students for careers in journalism and media, with a focus on multilingual reporting and communication skills.

Translation Faculty

Trains specialists in professional translation and interpretation across various languages, focusing on real-world applications and cultural fluency.
