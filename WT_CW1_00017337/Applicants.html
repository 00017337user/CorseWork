Welcome to UzSWLU

Why Choose This University

UzSWLU specializes in training professionals in foreign languages and linguistics. It collaborates with over 60 universities worldwide, offering dual degrees and exchange programs.

Tuition Fees

Tuition fees vary by program. For example, joint educational programs cost approximately $2,000 per year. Payments are made in the national currency at the Central Bank's exchange rate.

Notes for Students

Students can study in Uzbek, Russian, or English with full-time, evening, and distance learning options. Entrance exams include language tests and interviews.

Alumni Club

UzSWLU maintains connections with alumni through events and collaborations. Graduates can pursue careers both domestically and internationally.

Scholarships

Students may qualify for grants based on exam results and competitive selection. The university also offers financial support and tuition discounts in some cases.

International Cooperation

UzSWLU collaborates with institutions in the USA, UK, Germany, France, and other countries. This includes dual degree programs, conferences, and inviting foreign specialists.
